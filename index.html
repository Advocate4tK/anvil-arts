Anvil Arts is Tod Smith — pastor, chaplain, and software developer based in New England. The name isn't a brand exercise. A smith works at an anvil. He takes raw material and shapes it into something useful, something lasting, something that does the job it was made to do.

That's what we do with software. You bring the idea — the raw, unformed thing you've been carrying around. We put it on the anvil and hammer it into something real.

We work with sports organizations, churches, and nonprofits because those are the people doing the most important work with the least resources. They deserve tools that actually work.
